Our Purpose and Mission

Our learning center aims to deliver quality education for the Foundation Phase - Grade 1 to 3. We offer a small, intimate and nurturing environment that encourages each child to grow, develop his and her creativity; have fun and develop a passion for learning! We believe that each child is unique and has the right to a quality education; to be treated with love, respect and understanding.

What we offer?

A CAPS "Foundation Phase" Curriculum through IMPAQ

Four compulsory subjects

    > At least one home language ( English or Afrikaans)
    > A first Additional Language ( English, Afrikaans, and/or isiZulu
    > Mathematics
    > Life Skills
